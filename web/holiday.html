HOIDAY SPOTS AROUND KOLKATA

Santiniketan-Abode of Rabindranath Tagore

When it comes to excursions in Calcutta, the mention of Shantiniketan is like a must. Shantiniketan in Calcutta, is mainly known for Vishva-Bharati University that provides a platform for the convergence of western and eastern culture. Its foundation was laid by Nobel laureate Rabindranath Tagore.
How to reach:180 km from Kolkata.Driving Time: Around 3 hours.Can be reached by:Rail/ Road
Places of Interest:Shantiniketan has a number of other important cultural and heritage features too. The Uttarayan complex has several buildings like Konark, Udayan, Shyamali and others.The Rabindra Bhaban Museum also called Bichitra has many preserved letters, manuscripts, medals, documents, paintings and certificates and achievements associated with Tagore.


Digha

Literally the most sought after beach holiday retreat near Kolkata, Digha is located in the East Midnapur district at the border with Odisha. Lined with swaying chains of Casuarina trees, the beaches of Digha offer a beautiful canvas to be witnessed by the lovers of nature and serenity. Digha is mainly divided into Old Digha and New Digha. While Old Digha is found today at ruins and visited in the evening by lovebirds, New Digha is comparatively fresh in looks and attracts tourists for its Casuarina lined sea shore.
How to Reach: To travel to Digha from Kolkata, it takes around 4 hours. On most days, the Duronto Express leaves Howrah at 11:15 am and arrives at Digha in 2 hours and 30 minutes.s


Mandarmani-The Goa of the East

Another much celebrated beach destination near Kolkata, Mandarmani has been attracting tourists for last couple of years for its clean and wide sea shore. An ideal getaway to take refuge in the lap of nature, this golden sea beach is lined with lush green vegetation and exclusive beach resorts. The cleanliness of the beach would let the willing travellers to take a dip in its water.
Places of Interest:One can also rent a car and drive along the beach line to visit other nearby beach destinations like Tajpur, Digha, Dhankarpur, Talsharee, Udaypore and Junput. An interesting feature of the beach that is absolutely fascinating is the hundreds of tiny red crabs that climb up shore on the yellow sands.
