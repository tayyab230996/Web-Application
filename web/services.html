Our Services

We at Kolkata City Tours operate private and small group tours in and around the Kolkata city. All our tours are guided tours in Air Conditioned cars and buses, where guests get the opportunity to see the city through the eyes of real Kolkatans.Kolkata City Tours is proud to be a specialized service and the owners have been providing service and creating friendships that cannot be matched by any large-scale corporate tours. We feel that the best way to experience the “City of Joy” is hanging out with someone who’s knowledgeable and accommodating. Our guides will provide you with an experience that cannot be achieved by sitting on a bus with scores of other people.
