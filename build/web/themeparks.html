THEME PARKS IN KOLKATA

Nicco Park

Location: Sector IV,Salt Lake City, Kolkata-700106,West Bengal

A joint venture of Nicco Corporation and the Government of West Bengal, Nicco Park features over 35 attractions. Among the rides, Water Chute, Cable Car, Water Coaster, Family Carousel, Pirate Ship, Toy Train, Striking Cars and Bumping Boat are worth trying. Merry Go Round, Water Merry Go Round, Caterpillar and Twist-&-Turn are rides that children won't want to miss. For an utterly exhilarating experience, try 'Cyclone - The Roller Coaster' ride - the biggest wooden roller coaster in the country that runs on a 750-metre long track. 'Scaree'- the ghost house and 'River Cave Ride' are other rides that would definitely give you a hair-raising experience.

Aquatica Water Park

Location: Hatgachia,24 Parganas(S),Kolkata-700156,West Bengal

One-of-its-kind in the whole of eastern India, this unmissable attraction is located strategically at Kochpukur, close to Kolkata New Township. Approximately 75,000 sq-ft of the park is the pool area that offers you a range of water rides and slides.Some of the many exciting water rides here are Black Hole, Surf Racer, Tornado, Wave Pool, Mountain River and Mississippi Ride. One of the most enthralling features of Aquatica Water Park is the beautiful dance floor named 'Aqua Dance' that is fitted with water jets on the surface.


Science City

Location: J.B.S Haldane Avenue, Kolkata-700046, West Bengal

Millennium Park

Location: Strand Road,Fairley Place,Kolkata-700001,West Bengal

One of the highlights of the park is its proximity to the river. Greenery abounds in the park in the form of trees, small plants and properly manicured lawns. Amidst landscaped gardens and amusement rides for children, there are benches where you can unwind. You can get an undisturbed view of the complete Howrah Bridge from this park.
